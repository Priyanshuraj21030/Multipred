Why this matters ?

Diabetes and Breast cancer are related because high blood sugar levels in the blood of people with diabetes can damage blood vessels and the nerves that control them. This can increase the risk of cardiovascular disease, which is a term for heart disease and stroke. People with diabetes are more than double the risk of developing cardiovascular disease than people who don’t have diabetes and at a younger age. The longer you have diabetes, the more likely you are to have heart disease.
