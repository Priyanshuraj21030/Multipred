Detailed Disease Mechanisms

Primary Mechanisms

  • Hyperinsulinemia:

    Elevated insulin levels stimulate insulin and IGF-1 receptors, promoting cell proliferation and inhibiting apoptosis.

  • Metabolic Dysregulation:

    Altered glucose metabolism in cancer cells leads to increased energy availability for tumor growth.

  • Inflammatory Response:

    Chronic inflammation associated with diabetes creates an environment conducive to cancer development.

Hormonal Pathways

  • Estrogen Signaling:

    Insulin resistance affects estrogen production and bioavailability, potentially increasing breast cancer risk.

  • Growth Factors:

    Elevated insulin levels increase IGF-1 production, creating a pro-growth environment.

Prevention Strategies

Early Detection

  • Regular health screenings every 6 months
  • Daily blood sugar level monitoring
  • Monthly breast self-examinations
  • Annual mammograms after age 40
  • Regular consultations with healthcare providers

Lifestyle Modifications

  • Maintain BMI within healthy range (18.5-24.9)
  • 150 minutes of moderate exercise weekly
  • Balanced diet rich in fruits and vegetables
  • Limit processed foods and sugary drinks
  • Stress management techniques
